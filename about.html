About VantisCorp

Travel management, fully autonomous.

We're building the AI operating system that lets travel management companies scale revenue without scaling headcount. The next decade of growth shouldn't come at the cost of margin or service.

Built by travel-tech operators
Our mission

Make every TMC workflow self-driving.

For two decades, TMCs have stretched legacy GDS infrastructure thin. They add consultants every time bookings grow. VantisCorp is the AI layer that takes routine work off the human queue, so your team can focus on what humans actually do better.

Our vision

Scale on software, not seats.

A near future where a 50-person TMC operates at the scale of a 500-person one, with better service, tighter compliance, and faster financial close. The autonomous TMC isn't a slide deck. It's the product we ship.

What we believe

Six principles we build by.

01

Autonomy over assistance.

Copilots and chatbots leave the work on the human. Real value comes from agents that close the loop. Search, decide, execute.

02

Policy is the product.

Every TMC is unique because every customer policy is unique. We don't flatten that. We make policy first-class infrastructure.

03

Audit everything.

Autonomous agents only earn trust if every action is observable, explainable, and reversible. We log it all by default.

04

Speed to live.

Four weeks to go live, or we haven't earned the deal. Our integration layer reflects that promise.

05

Built with the industry.

We ship with TMC operators in the room, from solution design to weekly product reviews. Their queue, their problem, their input.

06

Humans where it matters.

Travelers in a crisis don't want a bot. Agents handle scale. Your consultants handle empathy. Both get their best work.

Our story

From travel-tech operators to autonomous TMC.

2024

The thesis.

After years inside travel-tech (building hotel mapping, room mapping, and revenue products), our founders saw the same pattern at every TMC. Huge booking volume, hand-cranked workflows, an industry papering over the gap with consultants. The autonomous-TMC thesis was born.

2025

First agents shipped.

Booking and Compliance agents went live with three pilot TMCs across India, the UK, and the US. Within 90 days, partners reported 50%+ faster booking turnaround and 4x faster approval cycles.

2026

Full platform.

Finance and Servicing agents launched. VantisCorp now manages 11K+ bookings monthly across 22+ TMCs in 11+ countries. New agent capabilities ship every two weeks.

What's next

Deeper NDC, mobile, and supply.

Deeper NDC, traveler-side mobile experiences, and a fifth agent dedicated to revenue and supplier negotiation. The autonomous TMC is just getting started.
