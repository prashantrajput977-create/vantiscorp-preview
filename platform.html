Policy enforced in real time. Every decision traceable.

Compliance Agent watches every booking and servicing action against your live policy. It blocks the wrong calls, routes approvals with full context to the right person, and writes an immutable audit log your finance and legal teams can export in one click.

Servicing Agent

Autonomous changes, cancellations, and rebookings.

Servicing Agent handles the traveler over WhatsApp, email, or chat. It rebooks delayed flights, processes refunds inside fare rules, and only escalates the complex cases. Your consultants get back the hours they used to spend on routine queue work.

Finance Agent

From PNR to posted journal. Books closed in hours.

Finance Agent matches airline, hotel, and vendor invoices to PNRs, handles GST, VAT, and sales tax, and posts clean journals straight into NetSuite, QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, or Tally. It also flags anomalies and overbilling before they hit your P&L.

Three-way match PNR, invoice, and payment reconciled automatically with exception flags
Tax and compliance GST, VAT, and sales-tax handling per market, audit-ready
ERP-native posting Journals land directly in your finance system. No CSV imports, no copy-paste
