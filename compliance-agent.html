What it does

Policy as code — applied automatically.

Pre-ticket exception detection

Identifies out-of-policy fares, hotel rates, and travel windows before money changes hands. Stops surprises at the source, not after the fact.

Context-rich approval routing

Routes to the right approver based on traveler, dept, cost center, and exception type — with the full trip context attached. Zero email back-and-forth.

Smart override rules

Auto-approves VIP overrides, repeat-traveler exceptions, and pre-cleared scenarios. Your finance team only sees what actually needs a human decision.

Live audit log

Every decision — who, when, why — recorded immutably. Export to CSV, S3, Snowflake, or BigQuery on demand.

Custom policy library

Build policies by traveler type, geography, project, or cost code. Versioned, testable, and rollback-safe — like infrastructure-as-code.

Regulatory readiness

SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and ISO 27001 ready. Compliance teams audit at any time, without filing a ticket.
