What it does

Servicing that scales — without scaling the team.

Auto-rebook on disruption

Detects schedule changes, cancellations, and IRROPs the moment they hit. Rebooks within policy and notifies the traveler.

Conversational servicing

Travelers ask via WhatsApp, email, or chat — the agent understands intent and acts. No tickets, no portals.

Policy-aware refunds

Calculates fare rules, residual values, and refund eligibility automatically. Pushes refunds to your finance stack.

Smart escalation

Routes complex or high-empathy cases to your senior consultants — with full conversation history pre-attached.

Multi-channel handoff

Picks up a WhatsApp thread inside Slack or Teams without losing context. Travelers never repeat themselves.

Duty-of-care alerts

Pushes traveler safety updates — weather, strike, geopolitical — and offers rebooking options before the traveler asks.
