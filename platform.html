Under the hood

One system, not four point tools.

Every agent runs on shared infrastructure. That's why work flows cleanly from search to settlement, and every action is traceable end-to-end.

P

Policy Engine

Set fare caps, approval chains, preferred vendors, and exception rules in one place. Every agent enforces them automatically — and policy changes propagate instantly across every workflow.

A

Audit Log

Every agent action — search, decision, override, payment — recorded with full context. Immutable, exportable, and observable by your compliance team without filing a ticket. SOC 2 Type II ready, GDPR compliant.

I

Integration Layer

Pre-built connectors to GDSs, OBTs, mid-office, ERPs, and communication tools. New systems take days to wire up, not quarters. No rip-and-replace, no long systems-integrator projects.
