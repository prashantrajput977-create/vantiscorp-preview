What it does

From request to confirmation, fully autonomous.

Multi-source shopping

Queries Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport, NDC aggregators, and direct airline feeds in parallel. Returns one ranked list, not nine browser tabs.

Policy-first ranking

Sorts by your real preferences: negotiated rates first, in-policy options next, out-of-policy clearly flagged with reason codes.

Fare conflict resolution

Detects rate, refundability, and inventory conflicts across sources. Picks the best ticket-able option automatically.

Profile-aware booking

Honors traveler loyalty numbers, seat preferences, meal codes, and corporate IDs without anyone touching a PNR.

Hotel and car continuity

Builds full trips, not just flights. Applies chain-level negotiated rates and ground transport policy in one pass.

Human-in-the-loop fallback

VIP overrides, complex multi-city, or low-confidence cases get handed to your consultant with full search history attached.
